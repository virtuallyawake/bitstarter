What is Kanban?

Kanban is a system for lean and just-in-time production developed at Toyota. Applied to software development, Kanban let's you streamline your development process for faster and more efficient development cycles.

Kankan implements the Kanban methodology as an app so that you and your team can always be on the same page.

Kankan: Kanban in an app

There are so many things to do when you work on a project that it is easy to lose track. Tasks get forgotten or relegated, leading to delays and wasted time.

Kankan can help you manage all your tasks so that your team can run like a well-oiled machine where everybody knows what they should be working on.

FAQ

How does Bitbucket fit into all of this?
Bitbucket is a code hosting service. It gives you an issue tracker with every repository. Kankan gets the tasks that your team is working on from the issue tracker for your project. That way, the team can continue using the issue tracker that they are already familiar with. Kankan let's you apply the Kanban methodology to the issues in the Bitbucket issue tracker.
Why Bitbucket and not GitHub?
Just to give back to Bitbucket for giving out free private repos. Yes, free!
